Here is what I've learned over the past couple of weeks or so trying to really sell this car.

 

Just like when we bought them, NO ONE knows what they heck they are.  No one understands the technology and even Ford does a bad job of promoting it.  So the used market for this car is terrible.  I would suspect that a dealership who knew what this car was would be able to retail this car for 29-30k.  Therefore the wholesale price should be 25-26k - however, I've been pulling teeth to fight to get 22k for this ride.  I'm not going to let it go for 22k.  It's worth more to me as a third car than trying to buy a third car for 22k a lot less equipped.

 

The market clearly is not ready for this vehicle still.  The only way it will fetch 29-30k is from a consumer who understands the technology and doesn't just assume it's a "hybrid" those guys are out there, but the salesforce doesn't have a clue how to sell this product , New or Used.

 

Combine all that with gas hovering around $2.00/gallon and basically the car has very little value in the used market.  Two dealerships flat out told me they didn't even want it at 22k - they didn't want to hassle.
